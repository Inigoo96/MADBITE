Frequently Asked Questions

How can I redeem a promotional code?

You can redeem your promotional code when placing your order on our website or app. In the shopping cart, you'll find a field to enter the code. You can also show it when placing your order directly at the restaurant.

Can I combine multiple promotions?

No, promotions cannot be combined with each other. You can apply only one promotion or discount per order, unless otherwise specified in the terms of a specific promotion.

How does the points program work?

For every dollar spent, you earn 1 point in your account. You can check your points balance in the "My Profile" > "Loyalty Program" section. Once accumulated, you can redeem them for different rewards that you'll find in that same section.

Do promotions apply to delivery orders?

Most promotions are available for both in-restaurant dining and delivery orders, but some may be exclusive to one mode. You'll find this information detailed in the description of each promotion.
